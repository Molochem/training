===========================
Mastering Plone Development
===========================

:About: Current best practices of Plone development
:Level: Experienced

Mastering Plone is intended as a week-long training for people who are new to Plone or want to learn about the current best practices of Plone development.
It can be split in two trainings:

- A beginner training (2 to 3 days) that covers chapters 1-18.
- An advanced training (3 to 5 days) that covers the rest.
